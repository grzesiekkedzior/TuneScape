Licenses & Attribution

Please take a moment to read this page. Behind every track, cover, and biography displayed in TuneScape stands the work of real people and open communities — including the broadcasters, curators, and volunteers who run the internet radio stations you listen to every day.

This application uses publicly available music metadata and artwork provided by open services. We are grateful to the communities and organizations that make this data accessible.

MusicBrainz

Music metadata is provided by MusicBrainz, an open music encyclopedia maintained by a global community.
Database content is released under CC0 1.0 (Public Domain).

Note: The MusicBrainz website and wiki use CC BY-NC-SA 4.0, but the database itself is CC0.

Cover Art Archive

Album artwork is provided by the Cover Art Archive, a joint project of MusicBrainz and the Internet Archive.
Individual images may carry various Creative Commons licenses depending on their source. TuneScape displays this artwork for informational purposes only.

Wikipedia

Artist biographies are sourced from Wikipedia via the Wikipedia REST API.
Text is available under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0).

Wikipedia® is a registered trademark of the Wikimedia Foundation.

Radio Browser

Internet radio station data is provided by Radio Browser, a free and open community database of internet radio stations.
The API is free to use, community-driven, and maintained entirely by volunteers.

Without Radio Browser, TuneScape would have no stations to play. Every station entry, tag, and fix in that database was submitted by a real person who wanted to make internet radio more accessible to everyone.

If you enjoy TuneScape, consider contributing to Radio Browser by adding missing stations or correcting existing entries at radio-browser.info.

Icecast

Many of the radio stations available in TuneScape stream their audio using Icecast, a free and open-source media streaming server.
Icecast is developed and maintained by the Xiph.Org Foundation and released under the GNU General Public License v2 (GPL).

Without Icecast and the broadcasters who run it, the internet radio ecosystem as we know it simply would not exist. It has been the backbone of independent and community radio streaming for over two decades.

TuneScape does not operate any Icecast servers. All streams are provided by independent station operators and broadcasters.

Fair Use & Respect for Artists

TuneScape is a free, open-source, non-commercial application. All music, artwork, and metadata remain the property of their respective creators and rights holders. This application does not host, distribute, or reproduce any copyrighted audio content.

We encourage users to support artists by:

  • Purchasing music legally
  • Attending concerts and live events
  • Using official streaming platforms
  • Respecting copyright and licensing terms

Open databases like MusicBrainz and Wikipedia exist thanks to volunteers worldwide. Please use this data responsibly and contribute back where you can.

If you are an artist, rights holder, or contributor with concerns about your data, please refer to the respective service providers linked above.
